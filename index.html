FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about the Nairobi Business Growth Conference

Who should attend the Nairobi Business Growth Conference?

This conference is designed for ambitious entrepreneurs, business owners, founders, and senior executives in Kenya and East Africa who are committed to scaling their businesses. Whether you're leading a startup, a small-to-medium enterprise, or a larger corporation and are feeling stuck, seeking proven growth frameworks, or looking to build a powerful network, this event will provide the strategies and connections you need.

How long is the event and what's the schedule like?

It is an intensive, high-impact, one-day event on Sunday, November 16th, 2025, from 3:00 PM to 9:00 PM. The schedule is carefully crafted for maximum learning and networking, featuring a keynote address, focused breakout sessions, dedicated networking breaks, a premium networking meal, and an interactive fireside chat with top business leaders.

What is the ticket price?

Tickets for the Nairobi Business Growth Conference are $200 USD. This includes full access to all sessions, a premium networking meal, conference materials, and digital resources. We accept payment via credit/debit cards, bank transfer, and mobile payments. Seats are limited, so we encourage you to reserve your spot early.

Is the Nairobi Business Growth Conference really worth it?

Absolutely. This is not a theoretical seminar; it is a strategic investment in the future of your business. The condensed, action-oriented format is designed to deliver immediate, actionable value. The cost of the ticket is often recouped many times over by applying just one or two of the strategies shared, not to mention the potential partnerships and opportunities that arise from connecting with our curated audience of business leaders.

Is this an in-person or a virtual event?

This is an exclusive in-person event held at the prestigious JW Marriott Hotel in Nairobi. We believe the most powerful connections and transformative learning experiences happen through live interaction, which is why we've designed this as a high-energy, face-to-face gathering.

Why attend a live event when I can learn from books, videos, or online courses?

While books and online courses are valuable, a live event provides a unique, immersive experience that digital content cannot replicate. Here, you get direct access to speakers for real-time Q&A, the ability to network with and learn from hundreds of like-minded peers, and the focused, undistracted environment needed to break through mental barriers and commit to a new course of action.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

All ticket sales for the Nairobi Business Growth Conference are final. We do not offer refunds or money-back guarantees. We stand firmly behind the immense value and quality of the conference, which is meticulously designed to deliver actionable strategies and powerful networking opportunities that provide a significant return on your investment.
